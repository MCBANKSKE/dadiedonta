Our Recruitment Process

Transparent, efficient steps from Nairobi to Gulf placement

1

Candidate Registration

Submit your details, qualifications, and work experience through our Nairobi office or online portal. Initial screening ensures you meet basic requirements.

Document Verification

Our Kenyan team verifies all submitted documents for authenticity and compliance with Gulf requirements. This includes certificates, passports, and references.

2
3

Interview & Selection

Candidates undergo interviews with our Nairobi team and potential employers via video call. Successful candidates receive detailed job offers.

Visa Processing

We handle all visa formalities including medical tests at approved Kenyan centers and embassy submissions. Typically takes 4-6 weeks.

4
5

Deployment

Final travel arrangements from JKIA including flight tickets, airport pickup, and accommodation in host country. Our support continues post-arrival.

Why Our Process Works

  • Average placement time: 8-12 weeks
  • Clear communication at every stage
  • No hidden fees - compliant with Kenyan regulations
  • Dedicated case manager for each candidate
